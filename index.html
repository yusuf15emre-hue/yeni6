Common Questions

Questions we
hear most often.

Is it safe to have a hair transplant in Istanbul? +
Yes — provided you choose an accredited clinic with a qualified surgical team. Our partner clinics operate to international standards and treat thousands of international patients every year.
How long do I need to stay in Istanbul? +
Most patients stay 3–4 nights in total. One night before the procedure and two to three nights after for the initial post-operative review before flying home.
When will I see the final results? +
Initial shedding of transplanted hairs occurs in weeks 2–4 — this is completely normal. New growth begins around month 3–4, with full results visible at 12–18 months.
What if something goes wrong after I return home? +
Your coordinator provides 12-month support including video consultations, direct contact, and clear protocols for any concerns that arise after you return home.
How much time off work will I need? +
Most patients take 5–7 days off work. You should avoid strenuous activity for 2–3 weeks, but desk work can typically resume within a week.
Can I see results from previous patients? +
Yes. Message us on WhatsApp and we'll share documented results from patients with similar hair loss patterns to yours.
